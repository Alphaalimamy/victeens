Frequently Asked Questions

How are the teens' identities protected?

We use code names instead of real names to protect the teens' identities. All photos are carefully reviewed and faces are blurred when necessary to ensure privacy.

How much does sponsorship cost?

Sponsorship starts at KES 2,000 per month for specific needs (like healthcare) and goes up to KES 10,000 per month for full sponsorship. You can choose the amount that works best for you.

Can I communicate with the teen I sponsor?

Yes! You can send messages through our moderated system. All communication goes through our staff to ensure safety and appropriateness. You'll also receive regular updates about your teen's progress.

How long does sponsorship last?

You can sponsor a teen for as long as they are in our program, typically until they graduate at age 19. However, you can pause or cancel your sponsorship at any time with 30 days' notice.
