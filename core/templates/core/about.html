About Us

Our Story of Hope & Resilience

Empowering vulnerable children across Africa through shelter, education, and unwavering compassion.

Victory Teens Organization (VTO) is a U.S. and African-based nonprofit providing shelter, education, medical care, and emotional support to orphaned and vulnerable children. With branches in Sierra Leone and Ghana, VTO works across Western, Central, and Eastern Africa, breaking cycles of poverty, neglect, and abuse.

Our Mission

To provide shelter, sustenance, education, medical care, spiritual guidance, and emotional support to needy orphans and vulnerable children.

Our Vision

To promote the welfare of vulnerable children, protect them from social injustice, and support them in achieving their dreams and full potential.
