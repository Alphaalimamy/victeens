Frequently Asked Questions

How will my donation be used?

All donations to this campaign will be specifically allocated to {{ campaign.title|lower }}. We provide detailed reports on how funds are used and the impact they create.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Victory Teens Organization is a registered charity and all donations are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

What happens if the campaign doesn't reach its goal?

Every donation makes a difference, regardless of whether we reach the goal. Funds will still be used for the intended purpose, and any shortfall will be covered through our general fund or future fundraising efforts.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to donate anonymously during the donation process. Your name will not appear in donor lists or public acknowledgments.
